Youth Employment Start

Youth Employment Start (Y.E.S.) provides one-stop advisory and support services on employment and self-employment to young people aged between 15 and 29. It provides support to young people to start their career on right track, enhance their employability, facilitate them to access the latest labour market information and help them secure a firm footing in the labour market for sustainable development. To cater for the distinct needs of the young people, Y.E.S. provides a wide range of services including the following areas

  • Career Assessment

  • Career Consultation Service

  • Mock Interview Workshop

  • Career Talk

  • Job search facilities, recruitment days

  • Computer Zone

  • Value-added training

  • Professional Counselling Service

  • Design Corner, Business Room, Business workstation and Professional Legal and Accounting Advisory Service for young people who aspire to self-employment

Supplementary Labour Division

The Supplementary Labour Division (SLD) is the application office of the Enhanced Supplementary Labour Scheme which allows employers with genuine difficulties in finding suitable staff locally to apply for importation of workers at technician level or below. However, to ensure the priority of local workers in employment, and to safeguard their salaries and benefits, employers must accord priority to fill available job vacancies with local workers. For details, please visit the Enhanced Supplementary Labour Scheme website.

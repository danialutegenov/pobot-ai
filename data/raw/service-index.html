Employment Services

Youth Employment Division
operates Youth Employment Start to provide career advisory services, support services on employment and self-employment to young people; implements Youth Employment and Training Programme (YETP) to provide pre-employment and on-the-job training to young people.
Employment Services Division
provides free employment and recruitment services to job seekers and employers; implements the Employment Programme for the Elderly and Middle-aged , the Re-employment Allowance Pilot Scheme and the Racial Diversity Employment Programme
Employment Information and Promotion Division
promotes the department's employment services
Supplementary Labour Division
serves as the application office of the Enhanced Supplementary Labour Scheme
Selective Placement Division
provides free employment service to job seekers with disabilities and free recruitment service to employers; and implements the Work Orientation and Placement Scheme
Employment Agencies Administration
regulates employment agencies and attests employment contracts concerning local employees working outside Hong Kong for overseas employers
Greater Bay Area Youth Employment Division
Implements the Greater Bay Area Youth Employment Scheme.
Working Holiday Scheme
provides an opportunity for young people in Hong Kong to gain first-hand experience living and working in a foreign culture and broaden their horizons

Employment Information and Promotion Division

The Employment Information and Promotion Division is responsible for promoting the Labour Department's employment services and disseminating employment information to help job-seekers find jobs and employers recruit suitable employees. The major work of the Division includes:

  • planning and organising activities to promote Labour Department's employment services;
  • receiving and processing vacancy orders, gathering job vacancy information proactively by strengthening rapport with employers;
  • providing telephone employment and job referral services to registered job seekers; and
  • organising large-scale recruitment activities and administering special programmes to assist employers in recruiting staff and job-seekers in finding jobs.
